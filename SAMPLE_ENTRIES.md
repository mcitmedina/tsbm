## Style Guidelines

### Song Meanings
- 2-3 sentences each
- Focus on themes, emotions, and universal experiences
- Use evocative, poetic language
- Connect to broader human experiences

### Personality Titles
- Creative and memorable
- Combine contrasting or complementary elements
- Use "The [Adjective] [Noun]" format
- Should feel mystical and archetypal

### Traits, Strengths, Challenges
- Specific and actionable
- Balance positive and realistic
- Connect to song themes
- 3-4 items each

### Summary
- 3-4 sentences
- Horoscope-like tone (mystical, insightful, affirming)
- Weave both songs together
- End with "You were born to..." statement
- Make it feel personal and meaningful
